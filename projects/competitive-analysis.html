Competitive Research & Positioning Strategy

A role-aligned strategy project showing how I would approach competitive research, LinkedIn benchmarking, messaging analysis, and actionable recommendations for Sparq’s marketing team.

Core Positioning Insight

Sparq has an opportunity to stand apart by focusing less on generic “AI transformation” and more on production-ready AI, operational performance, workflow modernization, and the systems where business value is actually created or lost.

Research Objective

The goal of this project is to demonstrate how I would evaluate Sparq’s market landscape, identify messaging opportunities, and translate competitive research into content recommendations that strengthen brand visibility.

01

Market Positioning

Evaluate how Sparq explains its value compared to other AI, data, and digital engineering firms.

02

LinkedIn Benchmarking

Review cadence, post formats, engagement patterns, thought leadership themes, and audience relevance.

03

Messaging Gaps

Identify where Sparq can communicate with more clarity, specificity, and executive-level relevance.

04

Recommendations

Turn findings into practical content ideas, LinkedIn improvements, and thought leadership opportunities.

How I Would Analyze the Market

Step 1

Identify direct and adjacent competitors in AI consulting, digital engineering, data modernization, and enterprise transformation.

Step 2

Compare website messaging, service language, proof points, industry focus, and tone of voice.

Step 3

Benchmark LinkedIn strategy across post frequency, format, engagement, leadership presence, and content themes.

Step 4

Translate research into content opportunities Sparq could actually use.
