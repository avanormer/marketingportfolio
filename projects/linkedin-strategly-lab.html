B2B Content Strategy / Thought Leadership

LinkedIn Strategy Lab

A content strategy project focused on corporate LinkedIn growth, executive presence, employee advocacy, and B2B thought leadership.

Project Objective

The goal of this project is to show how Sparq could use LinkedIn to communicate sharper ideas, increase brand visibility, and translate complex AI and engineering work into accessible content.

Content Pillars

  • Operational AI: Posts explaining practical AI adoption beyond hype.
  • Margin Recovery: Content showing where systems create or lose economic value.
  • Leadership Voice: Executive posts that sound sharp, human, and strategic.
  • Employee Advocacy: Posts that help team members amplify Sparq’s expertise.

Why this matters for Sparq

This project directly mirrors the role: LinkedIn benchmarking, content drafting, corporate presence, leadership visibility, and practical recommendations.
