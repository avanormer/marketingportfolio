AI Image Generation / Marketing Experimentation

AI Content Experiments

Experimenting with AI-generated visuals to support B2B marketing concepts, campaign ideas, and more engaging content formats.

Experiment Focus

This project explores how AI image generation can support marketing content without making B2B visuals feel generic, overproduced, or disconnected from business outcomes.

What I Tested

  • Visual concepts for enterprise AI, workflow modernization, and connected data systems.
  • Prompt differences between abstract AI imagery and practical business visuals.
  • How AI-generated visuals can support LinkedIn posts and blog headers.
  • Where AI visuals help — and where they risk feeling too generic.

Why this matters for Sparq

Sparq specifically values people who explore and adopt AI tools. This project shows curiosity, experimentation, and the ability to evaluate AI outputs strategically instead of using them blindly.
